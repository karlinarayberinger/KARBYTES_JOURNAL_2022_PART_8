It is approximately 10:15AM Pacific Standard Time in Dublin, California as I write this note. I am laying in what remains of the diminishing shade of “The Bunny Bush” as the sun rises higher in the cloudless sky and as ants crawl across my sun damaged skin. No matter what time of day or night it is, the nearby 580 freeway always has cars flowing across it; creating a perpetual “white noise” and air quality which is not exactly refreshing to inhale.

Rather than attempt to stay here longer like I was tentatively planning on doing in order to update the first eight pages of Karlina Object dot WordPress dot Com, I am planning on taking the BART train back to Castro Valley and walking straight up Redwood Road until I get to Ramage Peak trail. There is some nice shady, quiet, and clean places to sit with decent AT&T mobile data service. I figured that such a relatively high quality environment is worth the time, energy, and sun damage it would take me to get there. Though this area in Dublin is fairly convenient for using my laptop when the sun is not so high in the sky and when the humidity is not too high, I think that getting away from all this noise, air pollution, ants crawling across my skin, and people will make doing my website more enjoyable.

I cannot help but notice that the voices which are beamed into my skull via what I hypothesize is microwave auditory effect are more abrasive to me here than they are in that forest. Since approximately 3AM last night, I have been hearing the same antagonistic, sexist, and plaintive female voice hollering at me nonstop; making it hard for me to complete my work related thoughts while she and her minions tell me that they are sick of listening to me talk to myself (even when I do not use my vocal chords nor move my lips) about my web project and that they want me to joke around and cater to an unsophisticated teenage audience.

I would rather be around a bunch of old nerdy people than a bunch of young trash talking people. It helps me feel better to imagine that I am at least 100 years old while disguised as a young adult. Until a female bodied human is no longer in its fertile years, that female bodied human (unless convincingly disguised as a transman) is frequently heckled by other people who feel the need to police her body and to capitalize and to be the sole proprietor of her sexual and reproductive capacities. I find it helpful to pretend that such female bodied humans are bullied into submission by a chauvinistic society which is designed to make it logistically and socially difficult to live alone. Hence, most of such female bodied humans seem to be chronically sleep deprived, ostracized, and intentionally worn down by other humans until those female bodied humans submit to a man’s authority. Hence, if she wants to be healthy, safe, and not visibly destitute, she is implicitly required to get and to keep a boyfriend (which means that a significant amount of her time and energy will be spent dealing with being cheated on by her boyfriend and dealing with the aftermath of one romantic relationship ending after another). Given such inconveniences, I would say that only by being single and childless can a human adult truly have control over its own life (and sufficient free time to do serious personal development and not just dabbling tepidly in things like hatha yoga, pottery, and journaling).

Unfortunately, being single is implicitly discouraged by a “misery loves company” and “might makes right” society. Singles tend to have more disposable income, more free time, better physical and mental fitness, and greater career satisfaction than their married with kids counterparts. I have personally seen married with kids types act the most belligerent, disrespectful of my boundaries, conflict mongering, and antagonistic of all types of people. I can tell that such people are jealous of me and are desperate to diminish my satisfaction in life to the extent that I consider myself to be perpetually at war with such types of people (and I try my best not to react in a destructive manner when they pester me).

It is not fair that I have to be deprived of a home because the neighborhood where I would be living seems to require that all residents and visitors be on the same wavelength (metaphorically speaking). Though I am intentionally exaggerating and maybe fabricating details of this story, I do think there is some truth to what I am saying. I am not asking anyone to agree with me. I do not expect people to want to read my diaries nor even finish my trains of thought.

What annoys me a lot about many people is how, unlike me, they seem to condone censoring and sabotaging me when they do not like the content of my message while I do my best not to infringe on any person’s civil liberties. To be blunt, I think that I am more empathic, globally minded, and focused on innovating solutions to global than are most people. I noticed that my favorite types of people tend to be science geeks and engineers. Such people tend to genuinely love what they do and their enthusiasm and happiness is contagious. Also, such people tend to be a lot less judgemental, snarky, and irrational than those who are not Science Technology Engineering and Math nerds. I do believe that every aspect of reality has the potential to be understood and even explained using words. I do not subscribe to the ideology that some aspects of reality are fundamentally unknowable. If someone does not know something, they potentially have more to learn about whatever that something is. Just because someone does not know something does not mean that that gathering further intel about that thing is impossible. Science is an ongoing pursuit. It is never done asking questions about how reality works because reality is presumably infinitely large.

Anyway, I do not mean to come across as some kind of thought leader. What I intend to come across as to the general public is some naturalistic philosopher who is obsessed with certain topics and hobbies and just wants to focus on those things to the exclusion of most other things (which makes me appear to be selfish, apolitical, antisocial, mean, stubborn, arrogant, and ungenerous to many people). I intend to make the intellectual property I publish free, open source, and accessible to the general public (but I do have a target audience which seems to be a rather small subset of the general human population: those I could consider to be my peers and those I consider to be either “above me” or else “below me” in terms of how sophisticated one is in terms of its intellectual development. I write for an audience which is smarter than me, dumber than me, and about as smart as me. I especially write for an audience which I think is roughly equal to my intelligence level).
